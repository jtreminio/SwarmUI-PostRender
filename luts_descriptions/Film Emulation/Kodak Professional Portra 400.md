# Film Emulation / Kodak Professional Portra 400

Overall character: deep clean blacks, stronger reds and cyans and teals, and softer yellows and ambers.

## Realistic Photographs

| Area | Excels at | Poor at | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Portraits and skin | warm daylight portraits, editorial beauty, and romantic lifestyle shots | cool-balanced studio skin and deeper complexions in dim light | Skin-like oranges gain warmth and presence, which flatters many portraits but can overstate already-rosy faces. Deeper skin in dim scenes may print heavier. |
| Nature and foliage | dense botanical scenes, wet forests, and vivid spring landscapes | scenes that depend on exact green separation | The LUT gives greens extra energy without fully breaking them apart, so foliage-heavy frames feel more alive. |
| Sky, water, and cool light | general sky work, cool fill light, and moderate coastal scenes | strict color-accurate cool scenes | Cool hues stay usable, but the LUT still imposes a visible bias on sky-water separation. |
| Warm materials and light | brick, terracotta, wood, sunset light, and warm practical interiors | product oranges, calibration targets, and synthetic yellows | Warm materials get extra density and separation, which reads as cinematic but not fully neutral. |
| Contrast and fidelity | general cinematic grading where you want a finished look without an extreme curve swing | strict reference or color-managed neutral workflows | The tonal move is moderate, but the LUT still bakes in a clear palette preference. |

## Illustrations

| Area | Excels at | Poor at | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Character art | painterly portrait art, cover illustrations, and expressive character work | makeup-accurate or color-scripted faces | The LUT makes skin and warm accents feel more authored, which is excellent for mood but less ideal for exact palette preservation. |
| Backgrounds and environments | storybook streets, nostalgic interiors, and warm-world backgrounds | environments that need fully neutral atmospheric color | Environment work succeeds best when the scene already leans toward the LUTs preferred palette instead of fighting it. |
| Accent colors | accent colors built around reds, cyans and teals, and magentas and violets | yellows and ambers when they need to stay bright and exact | Hue emphasis is not evenly distributed, so some accents get extra life while others are pushed into the background. |
| Line art and grayscale | line art, comic inks, and grayscale illustration with some room for stylization | assets that need absolutely untouched neutral ramps | The LUT does not disturb neutrals too aggressively, but it is still a look transform rather than a utility conversion. |
| Graphic and vector work | stylized poster and cover work | exact brand-color applications | Even in moderate cases, the LUT is still a creative grade rather than a neutral finishing step. |
