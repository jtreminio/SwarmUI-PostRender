# Look LUTs / Bleach

Overall character: deep clean blacks, gentle highlight rolloff, stronger blues and cyans and teals, and softer yellows and ambers.

## Realistic Photographs

| Area | Excels at | Poor at | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Portraits and skin | balanced lifestyle portraits, travel portraits, and general people photography | deeper complexions in dim light | Skin is smoothed and slightly desaturated, which helps mood but reduces cosmetic and complexion accuracy. Deeper skin in dim scenes may print heavier. |
| Nature and foliage | restrained parks, late-summer fields, and naturalistic outdoor scenes | scenes that depend on exact green separation | Green saturation is held in check, which keeps outdoor scenes tidy but reduces lushness. |
| Sky, water, and cool light | cold skies, twilight blues, ocean scenes, and steel-toned night work | strict color-accurate cool scenes | Blue holds up well or intensifies, which helps night and winter imagery, but any hue rotation can push skies away from strict realism. |
| Warm materials and light | muted architecture, dusty streets, and soft golden-hour materials | product oranges, calibration targets, and synthetic yellows | Warm tones are restrained, which suits faded scenes more than fiery sunsets or bright signage. |
| Contrast and fidelity | general cinematic grading where you want a finished look without an extreme curve swing | strict reference or color-managed neutral workflows | The tonal move is moderate, but the LUT still bakes in a clear palette preference. |

## Illustrations

| Area | Excels at | Poor at | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Character art | general character illustration that benefits from a cinematic pass | makeup-accurate or color-scripted faces | Character work stays broadly usable, but the LUT still overlays a clear finish over flat local color. |
| Backgrounds and environments | general background painting and environment concept work | environments that need fully neutral atmospheric color | Environment work succeeds best when the scene already leans toward the LUTs preferred palette instead of fighting it. |
| Accent colors | accent colors built around blues and cyans and teals | yellows and ambers when they need to stay bright and exact | Hue emphasis is not evenly distributed, so some accents get extra life while others are pushed into the background. |
| Line art and grayscale | line art, comic inks, and grayscale illustration with some room for stylization | assets that need absolutely untouched neutral ramps | The LUT does not disturb neutrals too aggressively, but it is still a look transform rather than a utility conversion. |
| Graphic and vector work | stylized poster and cover work | exact brand-color applications | Even in moderate cases, the LUT is still a creative grade rather than a neutral finishing step. |
