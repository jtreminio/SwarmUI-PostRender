# Look LUTs / Ochre

Overall character: deep clean blacks, mostly neutral mids with warm highlights, bright clean highlights, and stronger reds and magentas and violets.

## Realistic Photographs

| Area | Excels at | Poor at | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Portraits and skin | warm daylight portraits, editorial beauty, and romantic lifestyle shots | cool-balanced studio skin, deeper complexions in dim light, and makeup or wardrobe color reference | Skin-like oranges gain warmth and presence, which flatters many portraits but can overstate already-rosy faces. Deeper skin in dim scenes may print heavier. |
| Nature and foliage | dense botanical scenes, wet forests, and vivid spring landscapes | scenes that depend on exact green separation | The LUT gives greens extra energy without fully breaking them apart, so foliage-heavy frames feel more alive. |
| Sky, water, and cool light | cold skies, twilight blues, ocean scenes, and steel-toned night work | strict color-accurate cool scenes | Cool hues stay usable, but the LUT still imposes a visible bias on sky-water separation. |
| Warm materials and light | brick, terracotta, wood, sunset light, and warm practical interiors | product oranges, calibration targets, and synthetic yellows | Warm materials get extra density and separation, which reads as cinematic but not fully neutral. |
| Contrast and fidelity | fashion editorials, action stills, and bold commercial imagery | subtle documentary scenes and already-high-contrast captures | The LUT pushes separation and color energy, which creates impact quickly but can feel too assertive on gentle source material. |

## Illustrations

| Area | Excels at | Poor at | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Character art | painterly portrait art, cover illustrations, and expressive character work | palette-matched turnarounds and makeup-accurate or color-scripted faces | The LUT makes skin and warm accents feel more authored, which is excellent for mood but less ideal for exact palette preservation. |
| Backgrounds and environments | storybook streets, nostalgic interiors, and warm-world backgrounds | quiet low-energy backgrounds | Environment work succeeds best when the scene already leans toward the LUTs preferred palette instead of fighting it. |
| Accent colors | accent colors built around reds, magentas and violets, and blues | strict brand palettes and locked swatch sets | Hue emphasis is not evenly distributed, so some accents get extra life while others are pushed into the background. |
| Line art and grayscale | vintage print effects, soft posterization, and textured monochrome art | neutral manga tones, UI mockups, and crisp black-ink line work | Neutrals pick up a cast and the black point lifts, which softens grayscale material instead of keeping it clinical. |
| Graphic and vector work | poster design, splash art, and marketing key visuals | small icons, dashboards, and flat vector systems | The LUT creates immediate impact, which helps hero images but overwhelms clean design systems. |
