# Look LUTs / Enhance

Overall character: deep clean blacks, bright clean highlights, and stronger cyans and teals and magentas and violets.

## Realistic Photographs

| Area | Excels at | Poor at | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Portraits and skin | warm daylight portraits, editorial beauty, and romantic lifestyle shots | cool-balanced studio skin | Skin-like oranges gain warmth and presence, which flatters many portraits but can overstate already-rosy faces. |
| Nature and foliage | dense botanical scenes, wet forests, and vivid spring landscapes | scenes that depend on exact green separation | The LUT gives greens extra energy without fully breaking them apart, so foliage-heavy frames feel more alive. |
| Sky, water, and cool light | cold skies, twilight blues, ocean scenes, and steel-toned night work | strict color-accurate cool scenes | Blue holds up well or intensifies, which helps night and winter imagery, but any hue rotation can push skies away from strict realism. |
| Warm materials and light | muted architecture, dusty streets, and soft golden-hour materials | scenes where warm brand colors must stay exact | Warm subjects stay attractive, though the LUT still nudges them toward its preferred palette. |
| Contrast and fidelity | fashion editorials, action stills, and bold commercial imagery | subtle documentary scenes and already-high-contrast captures | The LUT pushes separation and color energy, which creates impact quickly but can feel too assertive on gentle source material. |

## Illustrations

| Area | Excels at | Poor at | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Character art | painterly portrait art, cover illustrations, and expressive character work | strictly neutral character design sheets | The LUT makes skin and warm accents feel more authored, which is excellent for mood but less ideal for exact palette preservation. |
| Backgrounds and environments | storybook streets, nostalgic interiors, and warm-world backgrounds | quiet low-energy backgrounds | Environment work succeeds best when the scene already leans toward the LUTs preferred palette instead of fighting it. |
| Accent colors | accent colors built around cyans and teals, magentas and violets, and blues | strict brand palettes and locked swatch sets | Hue emphasis is not evenly distributed, so some accents get extra life while others are pushed into the background. |
| Line art and grayscale | line art, comic inks, and grayscale illustration with some room for stylization | assets that need absolutely untouched neutral ramps | The LUT does not disturb neutrals too aggressively, but it is still a look transform rather than a utility conversion. |
| Graphic and vector work | poster design, splash art, and marketing key visuals | small icons, dashboards, and flat vector systems | The LUT creates immediate impact, which helps hero images but overwhelms clean design systems. |
