# Look LUTs / Brooklyn

Overall character: controlled blacks, gentle highlight rolloff, and softer magentas and violets and cyans and teals.

## Realistic Photographs

| Area | Excels at | Poor at | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Portraits and skin | balanced lifestyle portraits, travel portraits, and general people photography | makeup or wardrobe color reference | Skin-like oranges gain warmth and presence, which flatters many portraits but can overstate already-rosy faces. |
| Nature and foliage | dry grass, dusty roads, and landscapes that benefit from controlled greens | lush tropical foliage and bright emerald landscapes | Green saturation is held in check, which keeps outdoor scenes tidy but reduces lushness. |
| Sky, water, and cool light | hazy coasts, subdued overcast, and calmer travel imagery | tropical cyan water, natural cobalt skies, and teal practical lighting | Cool hues stay usable, but the LUT still imposes a visible bias on sky-water separation. |
| Warm materials and light | muted architecture, dusty streets, and soft golden-hour materials | product oranges, calibration targets, and synthetic yellows | Warm tones are restrained, which suits faded scenes more than fiery sunsets or bright signage. |
| Contrast and fidelity | general cinematic grading where you want a finished look without an extreme curve swing | strict reference or color-managed neutral workflows | The tonal move is moderate, but the LUT still bakes in a clear palette preference. |

## Illustrations

| Area | Excels at | Poor at | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Character art | general character illustration that benefits from a cinematic pass | palette-matched turnarounds and makeup-accurate or color-scripted faces | Character work stays broadly usable, but the LUT still overlays a clear finish over flat local color. |
| Backgrounds and environments | general background painting and environment concept work | lush fantasy forests and cyberpunk teal scenes | Environment work succeeds best when the scene already leans toward the LUTs preferred palette instead of fighting it. |
| Accent colors | moderately saturated palettes that do not depend on one fragile hue | magentas and violets, cyans and teals, and yellows and ambers when they need to stay bright and exact | Hue emphasis is not evenly distributed, so some accents get extra life while others are pushed into the background. |
| Line art and grayscale | vintage print effects, soft posterization, and textured monochrome art | neutral manga tones, UI mockups, and crisp black-ink line work | Neutrals pick up a cast and the black point lifts, which softens grayscale material instead of keeping it clinical. |
| Graphic and vector work | retro poster treatments and mood boards | logo work, infographics, and palette-critical vector art | The transform is opinionated enough that clean graphic work stops looking intentionally flat. |
