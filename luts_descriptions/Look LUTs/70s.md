# Look LUTs / 70s

Overall character: lifted warm red shadows, mostly neutral mids with warm highlights, gentle highlight rolloff, and softer cyans and teals and yellows and ambers.

## Realistic Photographs

| Area | Excels at | Poor at | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Portraits and skin | warm daylight portraits, editorial beauty, and romantic lifestyle shots | cool-balanced studio skin and makeup or wardrobe color reference | Skin-like oranges gain warmth and presence, which flatters many portraits but can overstate already-rosy faces. |
| Nature and foliage | dry grass, dusty roads, and landscapes that benefit from controlled greens | lush tropical foliage and bright emerald landscapes | Green saturation is held in check, which keeps outdoor scenes tidy but reduces lushness. |
| Sky, water, and cool light | hazy coasts, subdued overcast, and calmer travel imagery | tropical cyan water | Cool hues stay usable, but the LUT still imposes a visible bias on sky-water separation. |
| Warm materials and light | muted architecture, dusty streets, and soft golden-hour materials | scenes where warm brand colors must stay exact | Warm subjects stay attractive, though the LUT still nudges them toward its preferred palette. |
| Contrast and fidelity | hazy window light, vintage travel frames, and soft atmospheric scenes | deep-black fashion, high-contrast noir, and crisp product photography | The tone curve lifts black and softens separation, preserving mood and detail at the expense of snap. |

## Illustrations

| Area | Excels at | Poor at | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Character art | painterly portrait art, cover illustrations, and expressive character work | palette-matched turnarounds and hard-edged cel-shaded sheets | The LUT makes skin and warm accents feel more authored, which is excellent for mood but less ideal for exact palette preservation. |
| Backgrounds and environments | storybook streets, nostalgic interiors, and warm-world backgrounds | lush fantasy forests and cyberpunk teal scenes | Earthy backgrounds benefit because greens are domesticated into a more vintage palette. |
| Accent colors | moderately saturated palettes that do not depend on one fragile hue | cyans and teals, yellows and ambers, and greens when they need to stay bright and exact | Hue emphasis is not evenly distributed, so some accents get extra life while others are pushed into the background. |
| Line art and grayscale | vintage print effects, soft posterization, and textured monochrome art | neutral manga tones, UI mockups, and crisp black-ink line work | Neutrals pick up a cast and the black point lifts, which softens grayscale material instead of keeping it clinical. |
| Graphic and vector work | retro poster treatments and mood boards | logo work, infographics, and palette-critical vector art | The transform is opinionated enough that clean graphic work stops looking intentionally flat. |
